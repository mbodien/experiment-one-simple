Welcome

For this HIT you will be taking part in an important research study about what makes memes resonate with people.

You will be asked to evaluate four different memes. The HIT will take about 4 minutes to complete.

Upon completion of the HIT, you will be given a reference code to put into MTurk.

We are interested in how different memes resonate with people. The memes being studied here include a message and an image of the person who said the message. The study will take no longer than 4 minutes.

Your data will be kept anonymous without information that could personally identify you. The data will be kept in a secure database, to which only the UCL researchers working with Paula Parpart have access. All data will be collected and stored in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.

It is up to you to decide whether or not to take part. If you choose not to participate, you won't incur any penalties or lose any benefits to which you might have been entitled. However, if you do decide to take part, you will be asked to agree to a consent form by ticking a box. Even after agreeing to take part, you can still withdraw at any time and without giving a reason.
